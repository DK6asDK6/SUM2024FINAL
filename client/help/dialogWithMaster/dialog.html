In my experience, everyone can see messages here, so how can we send personal messages to keep some secrets?


Sometimes it is needed to send personal message to get some secret information from master or from another player. In much more cases, it is needed for master to give personal information to player, for example, secrets told in personal way.
DK6Dragon's Dungeon supports this. Let's take an example:
.user:myFriend I send you personal message.
or:
.u:myFriend I send you personal message.
First, we initiate .user or .u command. Then we mention name of person who you are sending message like :username as it was shown.
Also you can send message to master even without his mentioning. There's another command:
.master I send personal message to Game Master.
or:
.m I send personal message to Game Master.
